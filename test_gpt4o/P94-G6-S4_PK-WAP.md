2. **Encouraging Meta-Reflection:** The AI missed opportunities to encourage the student to reflect on the overall system of their approximation, which could have facilitated Observing-level metacognition.

3. **Connecting to Broader Mathematical Structures:** The dialogue could have included prompts to connect the Taylor series work to broader mathematical theories, potentially moving towards Structuring.

4. **Exploring Alternative Methods:** The AI could have suggested exploring alternative approximation methods, such as path integrals, to foster Inventising-level thinking.

5. **Critiquing Assumptions:** The AI could have encouraged the student to critique their assumptions more rigorously, which might have led to deeper understanding and potential Observing-level insights.
